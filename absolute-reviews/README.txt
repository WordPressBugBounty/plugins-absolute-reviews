== Description ==

Absolute Reviews is a powerful, feature-rich WordPress plugin that helps you create beautiful, responsive, and modern review boxes for your website. Whether you're a professional critic, product reviewer, or just want to add ratings to your blog posts, this plugin has everything you need.

**Key Features:**

* **Multiple Rating Types** - Choose from percentage (1-100%), points (1-5), points (1-10), or star ratings (1-5) to match your review style.
* **Customizable Review Boxes** - Add headings, descriptions, criterion ratings, pros/cons lists, and more to your reviews.
* **Valid Schema Markup** - Automatically adds JSON-LD structured data to your reviews for better visibility in search results.
* **Gutenberg Block Support** - Easily add and customize reviews with dedicated Gutenberg blocks.
* **Auto-Calculate Total Score** - Option to automatically calculate the overall score based on individual criteria.
* **Pros & Cons Lists** - Add detailed pros and cons sections to provide balanced reviews.
* **Customizable Display Options** - Control typography, colors, and layout to match your website's design.
* **Review Posts Widget & Block** - Display collections of your reviews in various layouts with 8 different templates.
* **Mobile-Responsive Design** - Reviews look great on all devices and screen sizes.
* **RTL Support** - Full compatibility with right-to-left languages.

Absolute Reviews integrates seamlessly with WordPress and is perfect for review sites focusing on products, movies, books, restaurants, or any other reviewable content.

Enhance your content with professional-looking review boxes that engage your audience and improve your search engine visibility with Absolute Reviews!
